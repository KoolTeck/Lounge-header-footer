Our Mission

To bring people together in different localities towards living in a better, siren and life sustaining environments. At Lounge our core goal is to see to it that dilapidating infrastructures or non-existing amenities is shown out to the world and made public to gain government attention or the attention of great people that can help out. With lounge a better community is acheivable.
